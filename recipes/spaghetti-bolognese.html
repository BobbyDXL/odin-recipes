Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish made with ground beef, tomato sauce, and spaghetti. This recipe serves 4-6 people and can be customized to suit your taste preferences.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. In a large pot, cook ground beef over medium-high heat until browned, breaking it up into small pieces as it cooks.
  3. Add onion, garlic, carrot, and celery to the pot and cook until the vegetables are softened.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper to the pot and stir to combine.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Drain spaghetti and serve with the Bolognese sauce and grated Parmesan cheese.
