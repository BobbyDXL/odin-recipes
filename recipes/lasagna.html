Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. This recipe serves 4-6 people and can be customized to suit your taste preferences.

Ingredients

  • 8 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown ground beef in a skillet and drain excess fat.
  4. Add onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened.
  5. Combine marinara sauce, ricotta cheese, and Parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  6. Assemble the lasagna by layering noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture.
  7. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
